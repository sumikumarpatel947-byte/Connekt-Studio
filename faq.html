Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Connekt Studio and our services

How do I get started with Connekt Studio?

Getting started is easy! Follow these simple steps:

  1. Browse our class offerings and choose a plan that fits your needs
  2. Sign up for an account with your email and basic information
  3. Complete your payment securely through our payment gateway
  4. Check your email for login credentials and class schedule
  5. Join your first class using the provided Zoom/Google Meet link

New members also get a free orientation guidance to help you get familiar with our platform and answer any questions.

Do I need any prior yoga experience?

No prior experience is necessary! We welcome practitioners of all levels, from complete beginners to advanced yogis. Our classes are designed to be inclusive and adaptable.

We offer:

  • Beginner-friendly classes with detailed instructions
  • Modifications for different fitness levels and body types
  • Progressive learning paths to help you advance at your own pace
  • Private sessions for personalized guidance

What equipment do I need for online classes?

For most classes, you'll need minimal equipment:

  • Essential: Yoga mat, comfortable clothing, water bottle
  • Recommended: Yoga blocks, strap, blanket or cushion
  • Technical: Device with camera (laptop, tablet, or smartphone), stable internet connection

For specialized classes like prenatal yoga or therapy sessions, additional props may be recommended. Your instructor will provide specific guidance for each class type.

How do live online classes work?

Our live classes are conducted through Zoom and Google Meet platforms. Here's how it works:

  1. You'll receive a unique link for each class 30 minutes before start time
  2. Click the link to join the virtual classroom
  3. Your camera should be on so instructors can provide personalized feedback
  4. Classes are interactive - you can ask questions and receive real-time corrections
  5. All classes are recorded and available for 48-72 hours for review

We recommend joining 5-10 minutes early to test your connection and set up your space.

What if I miss a live class?

No worries! All live classes are recorded and available for replay:

  • Regular classes: Available for 48 hours after the live session
  • Workshop classes: Available for 72 hours after the live session
  • Course classes: Available until the end of your course period

You can access recordings through your member dashboard. We recommend watching within the availability period as recordings are automatically removed afterward.

Can I switch between different class types?

Yes! Our flexible membership allows you to explore different class types based on your membership level:

  • Beginner Plan: Access to beginner yoga and basic fitness classes
  • Intermediate Plan: Access to all regular classes including advanced yoga
  • Advanced Plan: Unlimited access to all classes including workshops and special programs

You can book different class types each week based on your schedule and preferences. We recommend trying various styles to find what works best for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods for your convenience:

  • UPI: Google Pay, PhonePe, PayTM, and other UPI apps
  • Credit/Debit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, American Express
  • Net Banking: All major Indian banks
  • Digital Wallets: PayTM Wallet, Amazon Pay, etc.

All payments are processed securely through PCI-compliant payment gateways. We never store your card details on our servers.

Can I cancel or change my membership plan?

Yes, you have full control over your membership:

  • Upgrade: Upgrade anytime - new benefits start immediately
  • Downgrade: Downgrade at the end of your current billing period
  • Cancel: Cancel anytime - access continues until the end of your paid period

To make changes, simply log into your account, go to "My Membership," and select your preferred option. Changes take effect according to the billing cycle.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we have a fair refund policy:

  • 7-Day Cooling Period: Full refund if you cancel within 7 days and haven't attended more than one class
  • Monthly Plans: Partial refunds available before the 15th of the month
  • Multi-Month Plans: Tiered refunds based on usage and time elapsed
  • Medical Emergencies: Special consideration with documentation

Please refer to our detailed Refund Policy for complete terms and conditions.

What are the technical requirements for online classes?

For the best experience, we recommend:

  • Device: Laptop, tablet, or smartphone with camera capabilities
  • Internet: Minimum 5 Mbps speed for smooth video streaming
  • Browser: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest versions)
  • Apps: Zoom or Google Meet (free accounts work fine)
  • Space: Enough room to lie down with arms extended

We also offer a technical test session before your first class to ensure everything works smoothly.

What if I have technical issues during a class?

We provide comprehensive technical support:

  • Live Support: Technical assistant available during class hours
  • Quick Solutions: Common issues resolved within 2-3 minutes
  • Backup Options: Phone-in option if video fails completely
  • Recording Access: Full access to class recording if you miss portions due to technical issues

For persistent issues, contact our support team at tech@connektstudio.com or call +91 98765 43210.

Is yoga safe for beginners and older adults?

Yes, yoga is generally safe and beneficial for all ages when practiced correctly:

  • Beginner Classes: Specifically designed for those new to yoga
  • Chair Yoga: Available for those with mobility limitations
  • Gentle Yoga: Perfect for older adults and those recovering from injury
  • Modified Poses: Instructors provide alternatives for all fitness levels

Important: Always consult with your healthcare provider before starting any new exercise program, especially if you have pre-existing health conditions.

What should I do if I have an injury or medical condition?

Your safety is our top priority. Here's how we accommodate special needs:

  • Health Disclosure: Inform us of any injuries or conditions during registration
  • Private Consultation: Free 15-minute consultation with our wellness experts
  • Customized Modifications: Personalized pose modifications for your condition
  • Specialized Classes: Therapeutic yoga and restorative sessions available

For serious medical conditions, we require doctor's clearance before participation. Our instructors are trained to work with various health concerns and will ensure your practice is safe and beneficial.

How soon will I see results from practicing yoga?

Results vary by individual, but here's what most people experience:

  • Immediate (1-2 weeks): Better sleep, reduced stress, improved mood
  • Short-term (1-2 months): Increased flexibility, better posture, more energy
  • Medium-term (3-6 months): Significant strength gains, weight management, mental clarity
  • Long-term (6+ months): Deep transformation, sustainable lifestyle changes

Consistency is key! Regular practice (3-4 times per week) yields the best results. Remember that yoga is a journey, not a destination - enjoy the process of growth and self-discovery.
