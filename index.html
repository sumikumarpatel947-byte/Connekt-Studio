Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Connekt Studio

How do I get started with Connekt Studio?

Starting is very easy! Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Look at our classes and pick a plan you like
  2. Create an account with your email
  3. Pay safely using our payment system
  4. Check your email for login details and class times
  5. Join your first class with the link we send you

New members get a free session to help you learn how everything works!

What equipment do I need for online classes?

For most classes, you need:

  • A yoga mat (very important)
  • Comfortable clothes for exercise
  • Good internet connection
  • A phone, tablet, or computer with camera
  • Water and a towel

For harder classes, you might want yoga blocks or light weights. But our teachers always show easier ways if you don't have equipment.

What types of classes do you offer?

We have many different classes for everyone:

  • Yoga for Beginners: Perfect if you're new to yoga
  • Power Yoga: Fast and challenging classes
  • Meditation: Help you relax and feel calm
  • Strength Training: Make your whole body stronger
  • Stretching: Help you become more flexible
  • Therapeutic Yoga: Help with injuries or health problems

What are your pricing options?

We have different prices to fit your budget:

  • Monthly: ₹1,499 per month - All classes included
  • 3 Months: ₹3,999 - You save 11%
  • 1 Year: ₹12,999 - You save 28%
  • One Class: ₹299 - Pay only when you come

All plans let you watch class recordings again and get personal help from teachers.

What if I miss a live class?

No problem! We record all live classes and you can watch them for 7 days. You can:

  • Watch the recording anytime for 7 days
  • Practice when you have time
  • Watch difficult parts again
  • Still ask questions to teachers online

Is online yoga safe for beginners?

Yes, online yoga is very safe when done right. Here's how we keep you safe:

  • Classes for beginners with clear instructions
  • Teachers show easier ways for all fitness levels
  • We focus on doing poses correctly to avoid injury
  • We ask about your health before you start
  • You can turn off camera if you feel shy

We always suggest talking to your doctor before starting any new exercise, especially if you have health problems.
