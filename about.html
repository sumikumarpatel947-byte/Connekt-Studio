About Connekt Studio

Where ancient wisdom meets modern wellness to transform your life

Our Story

From a small yoga room to a global wellness community

Founded in 2018, Connekt Studio began as a humble yoga room in Mumbai with just 12 students. Our founder, Priya Sharma, envisioned a space where ancient yogic wisdom could blend seamlessly with modern fitness science to create transformative wellness experiences.

What started as weekend yoga classes quickly evolved into a comprehensive wellness platform. Today, we serve over 10,000 students across 15 countries, offering live online classes, personalized fitness programs, and a supportive community that extends far beyond the yoga mat.

Our mission remains simple: to make authentic yoga and holistic wellness accessible to everyone, regardless of their location, experience level, or physical abilities.

Our Philosophy

The principles that guide everything we do

Authenticity

We honor the ancient traditions of yoga while embracing modern scientific understanding. Our teachings are rooted in authentic yogic principles passed down through generations.

Inclusivity

Yoga is for everyone. We create welcoming spaces where people of all ages, body types, fitness levels, and backgrounds can practice safely and joyfully.

Holistic Wellness

True wellness encompasses body, mind, and spirit. Our integrated approach addresses physical health, mental clarity, emotional balance, and spiritual growth.

Continuous Learning

We are committed to ongoing education and growth. Our instructors continuously update their knowledge and skills to bring you the best in wellness education.

Community

We believe in the power of connection. Our supportive community provides encouragement, accountability, and friendship on your wellness journey.

Sustainability

We are committed to environmental stewardship and sustainable practices that honor our connection to the planet and future generations.
